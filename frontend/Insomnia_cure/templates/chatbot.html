When to See a Doctor

Consult a healthcare provider if you experience:

  • Persistent difficulty sleeping for over a month
  • Daytime fatigue affecting work or driving
  • Loud snoring with pauses in breathing
  • Uncomfortable sensations in your legs at night
  • Sleep problems accompanied by other health issues

This assistant provides general information only, not medical advice.
